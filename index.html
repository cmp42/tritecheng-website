About Tritech

Tritech Engineering Corporation was founded in 1992 by Robert J. Stowell. At Tritech, our emphasis is on client service and satisfaction. Clients can expect our project managers to use the seasoned judgment necessary to make swift and necessary decisions. This effective practice ensures direct lines of communication, management accountability, and that client service is our ultimate priority.

The Philosophy

Tritech Engineering Corporation is a personal service firm that provides objective and professional engineering, surveying, and land planning. The foundation of our work is the understanding of, and the commitment to, the balance of management and technical expertise. Our knowledge and respect of real property development is essential to the problem solving that provides not only a technically correct design, but one that provides a cost effective solution and meets your needs.

The Technology

At Tritech our engineers use the latest engineering, drafting, and modeling software to generate the most comprehensive plans available. Tritech plans and reports are clear, concise, and above all, accurate. Tritech Engineering surveyors are armed with the most accurate, reliable and easy to use equipment available. Our surveyors have the training and experience necessary to use this equipment to its fullest and generate the best product possible for our clients. Ultimately, this technology allows Tritech to provide a more accurate and more cost effective product for the client.

The Experience

Company ideals and technology are only a part of the picture. Each of the Tritech Engineering staff members has several years of experience in their areas of expertise— a certain asset they bring to the company. Most of all, they each bring commitment and genuine caring for perfecting their craft and client satisfaction.
