Commercial Engineering Projects in Dover, New Hampshire

Tritech Engineering has completed commercial engineering projects throughout Dover, NH and the Seacoast region. Our experience includes medical offices, mixed-use developments, and complex commercial sites requiring advanced stormwater management, grading, and utility coordination.

Exit 9 Medical Office Site

15-Acre Commercial Medical Development • 100,000+ Sq. Ft. Facility • Dover, NH

The Exit 9 Medical Office Site includes a 100,000+ square foot medical office building with under-building parking, multiple parking areas, and extensive site utilities. The project required detailed grading, utility coordination, and advanced stormwater management design.

Key Design Elements:

  • 10,000 cubic foot under-pavement stormwater detention and infiltration system
  • Innovative stormwater treatment strategies
  • Integrated under-building parking structure
