Municipal Projects

Gerrish Commons

Somersworth, New Hampshire • 29-Lot Municipal Open Space Subdivision

Gerrish Commons is a 29-lot open space subdivision coordinated closely with municipal review authorities. Designed to preserve natural land while providing efficient municipal infrastructure, the project represents a strong collaboration with the City of Somersworth.

Key Design Elements:

  • Cluster-lot open space subdivision
  • Municipal coordination throughout design
  • 21.8 acres of 32.3 acres placed in conservation
  • 165 GPM municipal dry-well pump station
