Municipal Engineering Projects in Dover, NH

Tritech Engineering has completed municipal engineering projects for towns and cities throughout Dover, NH and surrounding communities. Our work includes public infrastructure, open space subdivisions, and projects requiring close coordination with municipal review boards and agencies.

Gerrish Commons

Somersworth, New Hampshire • 29-Lot Municipal Open Space Subdivision

Gerrish Commons is a 29-lot open space subdivision designed in close coordination with municipal review authorities. The project preserves natural land while providing efficient municipal infrastructure and reflects a strong collaboration with the City of Somersworth.

Key Design Elements:

  • Cluster-lot open space subdivision design
  • Municipal coordination throughout planning and review
  • 21.8 acres of 32.3 acres placed in permanent conservation
  • 165 GPM municipal dry-well pump station
