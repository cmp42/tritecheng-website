Working at Tritech Engineering

Every day, we strive to deliver high-quality service through clear communication and strong coordination with our clients, outside contractors, and municipal teams. Our decades of experience allow us to problem solve effectively and navigate the strict and often complex Town, State, and Federal regulations with confidence. As a small business, each employee truly becomes part of the family (especially if you love the office dogs as much as we do!). Our office dogs are part of the culture, and we believe in a relaxed, positive environment where people feel comfortable, productive, and valued. We pride ourselves on offering a supportive, hands-on work environment with a healthy work-life balance and meaningful opportunities for growth.

Because of our team size, every employee receives individual mentorship and exposure to a wide range of real-world projects - from engineering design and stormwater management to surveying, land planning, and development. Whether you are looking to try on multiple hats or specialize in the areas you enjoy most, Tritech provides the flexibility and guidance to help you thrive professionally.

Tritech is always open to bringing on motivated and hardworking individuals. If you believe you would be a great fit, we encourage you to reach out. We are happy to review resumes and connect confidentially.
