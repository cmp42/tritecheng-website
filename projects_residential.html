Residential Engineering Projects

Tritech Engineering has completed residential engineering and site development projects throughout Dover, NH and the surrounding Seacoast region. Our work includes subdivisions, roadway and utility design, stormwater planning, and mixed-use residential communities.

Thornwood Commons

Dover, New Hampshire • Mixed-Use Residential Community • 100+ Acre Site

Thornwood Commons is a 100+ acre redevelopment that includes a large residential component featuring 48 energy-efficient, green-design, age-restricted single-family homes. The project integrates residential living with commercial and congregate care facilities while preserving natural features.

Key Design Elements:

  • 100K+ sq. ft. retail space
  • 100K+ sq. ft. office space
  • 112-unit congregate care facility
  • Energy-efficient “green home” design
  • 3,700 ft public roadway
  • Two 10-foot span arched culverts
  • Pond reutilized as a detention basin
  • Dam Breach Analysis
